I'm Akshay — a Chartered Accountant who spent a decade inside the numbers before writing about them.

Ten years across corporate finance and boardroom reporting. I qualified as a CA in India, worked across financial planning and capital allocation — the kind of work where the spreadsheet is never wrong, only the assumption is.

This site exists for a specific reader: an operator, founder, or investor who has outgrown beginner content but doesn't want academic theater. Someone who can read a cash flow statement and wants to think harder, not just read more.
